The Best Movies according to Jeremias Avalis

My top 3 movies of all times

Hombre en llamas

The movie has intense action sequences and gripping storylines. If you appreciate adrenaline-pumping scenes, it delivers them with their high-stakes action and suspense.

Gladiator

It features a strong character development. You may be drawn to the compelling protagonist who undergo personal transformation throughout the story, and makes you emotionally invested in his journey.

Gran Turismo

The movie appeals to racing enthusiasts with its realistic graphics and attention to detail in replicating real-world cars and tracks.
